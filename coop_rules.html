Coup Rules (Regular Deck Version)

Coup is a game of bluffing, deduction, and strategy. This version uses a regular deck of cards, replacing the original characters with playing cards as outlined in the reference card.

Setup

  • Remove all cards except the ones listed in the reference card table. There are 3 copies of each card for a total of 15 cards in the deck.
  • Shuffle the deck and deal 2 cards to each player. These are their influence cards and should be kept face down.
  • Each player starts with 2 coins. The remaining coins form the "Bank."
  • Place the deck face down in the center to form the draw pile.

Objective

Be the last player standing by eliminating all other players’ influence cards.

Gameplay

  • On your turn, take one of the following actions:
    • Income: Take 1 coin from the Bank.
    • Foreign Aid: Take 2 coins from the Bank (can be blocked by "4").
    • Coup: Pay 7 coins to force a player to lose one influence. (If you have 10 coins, you must Coup.)
    • Character Action: Use the action of a card in your hand or bluff by pretending to have it. See the reference card for actions.
  • If a player challenges your action, reveal the relevant card. If you can’t, you lose one influence.
  • Discard revealed cards face up and draw replacements as necessary.

End of the Game

The game ends when only one player has influence cards remaining.
