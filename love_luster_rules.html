Love Letter Rules (Regular Deck Version)

Love Letter is a game of deduction, risk, and luck. This version allows you to play with a regular deck of cards, including the 6-player expansion with Spies.

Setup

  • Shuffle the deck and deal 1 card to each player. Place one card face down as the "Burn Card" (not used in the round).
  • For 2 players, deal 3 additional cards face up to the center (also not used).
  • The remaining deck becomes the draw pile.

Objective

Be the last player standing or have the highest-ranked card in hand when the deck runs out.

Gameplay

  • On your turn, draw a card and play one of the two cards in your hand.
  • Resolve the effect of the played card immediately.
  • The effects of each card are listed on the reference card.

End of the Round

  • If the deck runs out, all players reveal their cards. The highest-ranked card wins.
  • If all but one player is eliminated, the remaining player wins.
  • The winner receives a token of affection. The first player to earn a set number of tokens wins the game:
    • 2 players: 6 tokens
    • 3 players: 5 tokens
    • 4 players: 4 tokens
    • 5-6 players: 3 tokens

Card Effects

Card Effect
Ace (x1) You lose if you play or discard this card.
King (x1) Must be played if you also have a Queen or Jack.
Queen (x1) Trade hands with another player.
Jack (x2) Draw 2 cards. Choose one to keep and return the other to the bottom of the deck in any order.
10 (x2) Choose a player (including yourself) to discard their hand and draw a new card.
9 (x2) You are immune to effects until your next turn.
8 (x2) Compare hands with another player. The player with the lower-ranked card is eliminated (4s and 5s are counted as tied).
7 (x2) Look at another player’s hand.
4s and 5s (x6) Guess a player’s hand (not 4 or 5). If correct, that player is eliminated.
3 (x2) If you are the only player who played or discarded a 3 and you were not eliminated, gain 1 bonus point.
