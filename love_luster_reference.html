Love Letter Reference Card

Card Effect
Ace (x1) You lose if you play or discard this card.
King (x1) Must be played if you also have a Queen or Jack.
Queen (x1) Trade hands with another player.
Jack (x2) Draw 2 cards. Choose one to keep and return the other to the bottom of the deck in any order.
10 (x2) Choose a player (including yourself) to discard their hand and draw a new card.
9 (x2) You are immune to effects until your next turn.
8 (x2) Compare hands with another player. The player with the lower-ranked card is eliminated (4s and 5s are counted as tied).
7 (x2) Look at another player’s hand.
4s and 5s (x6) Guess a player’s hand (not 4 or 5). If correct, that player is eliminated.
3 (x2) If you are the only player who played or discarded a 3 and you were not eliminated, gain 1 bonus point.
